# PetPal Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 2026

PetPal ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how PetPal collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use the PetPal mobile application.

By using PetPal, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Personal Information

PetPal requires users to create an account to access the app features. When you register or log in, we may collect:

* Email address
* Basic account information necessary for authentication and account management

### b. Pet-Related Information

PetPal allows users to store and manage information related to their pets, including but not limited to:

* Pet name, age, and breed
* Weight and growth records
* Health records and vet visits
* Feeding schedules and food information
* Walk activities and exercise logs
* Medication and reminders

This information is provided by the user and is stored securely.

### c. Images

If you choose to upload images (such as pet profile photos), these images are stored and used only to support app functionality.

### d. Location Information

PetPal **does not collect, store, or track users’ precise location**.

The app uses Google Maps services solely to display nearby places such as veterinary clinics, pharmacies, parks, and pet stores. Any location data used for map display is handled directly by Google Maps and is not stored or processed by PetPal.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

* Create and manage user accounts
* Provide and maintain PetPal features
* Store and display pet-related data
* Improve app functionality and user experience
* Communicate important updates related to the app

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

PetPal uses trusted third-party services to operate core functionality. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies:

* **Google Firebase** (Authentication, Database, Storage)
* **Google Maps API** (Map display and place information)

PetPal does not sell or share personal data for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Advertising

PetPal does not display advertisements in the current version of the app.

If advertisements are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly to reflect any changes.

---

## 5. Data Security

We take reasonable and appropriate measures to protect your information using secure technologies and services. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain user and pet-related data for as long as the account is active or as necessary to provide the app’s services. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

PetPal is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

---
